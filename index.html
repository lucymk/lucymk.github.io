WHAT TO DO WITH
CARROT TOPS?

Carrot Top Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of carrot tops, picked and washed
  • 1/2 cup of nuts (you choose, pine nuts are a classic, walnuts also work well
  • Olive oil
  • Some hard cheese, grated (parmesan is traditional, but you can use others too)
  • Any other fresh herbs you have lying around and want to use up
  • Seasoning

Method:

  1. Toast the nuts lightly, in the oven or in a dry pan
  2. Depending how powerful your blender is, cut the carrot tops and nuts a bit
  3. Blend everything together with enough oil to make the consistency you like
  4. Season to taste and enjoy with pasta, sandwiches, wraps, crudites etc.

Carrot Top Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of carrot tops, picked, boiled for a few minutes and rinsed
  • 3 potatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • Vegetable stock (500 ml)
  • Oil (1 tbsp) and butter/ghee (1tsp)
  • Spices: cumin seeds (1/2 tsp), fresh ginger (1.5cm), 1 dried chilli, garam masala (1 heaped tsp), turmeric (1/2 tsp), 1 clove garlic, salt and pepper

Method:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan, when hot fry cumin seeds until fragrant
  2. Add onion and cook for few minutes before adding the spices
  3. Then add the potatoes and cook until slightly coloured
  4. Add the tomato and carrot greens and fry
  5. Add enough vegetable stock to cover and cook (adding more stock/water if needed) until potatoes are cooked and you like the liquidity

Carrot Top Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of carrot tops, picked and washed
  • 1 tin of chickpeas
  • 4 carrots, shredded
  • Breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 onion and 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • Seasoning (e.g. salt, pepper, lemon juice)

Method:

  1. Boil the carrot tops for a few minutes then let drain and press the water out
  2. Saute onion and carrot for a few minutes, then add garlic
  3. Put into food processor with the carrot tops, drained chickpeas, breadcrumbs, egg and seasoning
  4. Blend until it holds together but isn't completely smooth and check seasoning
  5. Refrigerate if time, then shape into burgers and cook in a pan for about 8 minutes each side.
