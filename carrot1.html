Recipe - Carrot Top Pesto

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch of carrot tops, picked, washed and roughly chopped
  • 2 handfuls of your choice of nuts (e.g. pine nuts, walnuts etc)
  • Oil (again, your choice: olive oil is always good!)
  • Some other herbs: a few leaves of basil, parsely, whatever you want to use up
  • Some hard cheese if you like (Parmesan is the classic choice, but you could try others too)
